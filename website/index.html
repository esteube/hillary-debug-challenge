About Me

Describe where you come from and who you are (hometown, favorites, siblings, hobbies, etc.)

I am originally from Los Angeles, California although I moved to Colorado when I was 25 years old. I have been in Colorado for over 25 years, so I have now lived half my life, so to speak in 2 different places! I have two grown children and a grandbaby on the way. my daughter is Kayla who is 23 and is a college student at Maharishi University of Management and a son who is 21, Dylan who is the one expecting and is studying at Colorado Tech University.

I have in the past been an avid skiier, although my newest hobby is road biking. I also enjoy hiking in the Colorado mountains or the small walk around lakes near Arvada where I live.

Camping is another adventure I enjoy pursuing and have dabbled with rock climbing starting with a course I took at Metro, which was one of the most fun classes I have taken.

One of my biggest love is for playing Irish Traditional Fiddle and I often play in sessions in Boulder and Denver weekly.

I have a flair for the creative and thus it has been fun and a labor of love to design and rennovate three bathrooms in my home with stone travertine and marble tile with glass block and high end faucet fixtures. I also designed a blown glass bowl as a sink for my master bathroom.

Career

Describe where you're going in life and who you will become (career goals, gSchool goals, hopes, dreams, etc)

For many of my adult years I have been a stay at home Mom raising my two beautiful children although for much of that time I had my hand in different entrepreneur endeavors. I have had many jobs and some businesses: Creative Memories, Massage Therapy, Data Base Coordinator at Lockheed Martin, Support Technical postitions at TRW, Skye Music Studios where I taught fiddle and sold violins and accessories. Skye Bobcat Services where I owned my own bobcat serving residential homes for landscaping, Freelance Web Designer - Web Server Admin & technical support and a Health and Wellness business.

Last May of 2014 I graduated from Metro State University with a Bachelors of Science in Web Development and Business Intelligence with a Math and Music Minor.

Most recently I have finished a Web Design class at gschool in Denver with Matt Leach. I loved it and learned many things with HTML, CSS and Web design in general including layout, some bootstrap with responsive layout, and we even touched on PHP for forms.

I have an interest in learning more about PHP and how it can be applied to create custom made websites in WordPress.
